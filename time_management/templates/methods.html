Eisenhower Method

This method allows you to organize your tasks into 4 quadrants sorting them by important vs unimportant and urgent vs not urgent

SMART

SMART analysis provides a simple and clear structure for defining goals and objectives. It can be used by anyone, anywhere, and does not require special skills in setting SMART tasks.

Pareto analysis

The 80/20 rule is the idea that 20% of actions are responsible for 80% of outcomes. It helps you prioritize tasks that will solve problems.
